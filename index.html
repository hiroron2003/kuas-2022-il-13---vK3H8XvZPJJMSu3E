About KUAS

KUAS is a university with five faculties: Social Sciences, Medical and Health Sciences, Humanities, and Natural Sciences.These five faculties are fused with each other and resonate with each other to provide a place where students can receive more advanced education, research, and extracurricular activities.Our goal is for each student to grasp his or her own dreams and goals, and to create a bright future like a rainbow.

Three strengths you can acquire at KUAS.

  • Practical English Proficiency
